The Best Movies according to Priyanshu

My Top 3 Movies of all time

1. MS DHONI: THE UNTOLD STORY

The MS Dhoni movie is considered one of the best due to its authentic portrayal of Dhoni's life, Sushant Singh Rajput's compelling performance, effective direction, engaging storytelling, and the emotional connection it establishes with the audience.



2. JOLLY LLB 2

The main theme of the movie "Jolly LLB 2" revolves around justice, legal ethics, and the fight against corruption in the Indian legal system. The protagonist, Jolly, takes on a high-profile case that challenges the flaws within the judicial system and aims to bring about positive change.



3. 12TH FAIL

"Never Give Up" is the essence of 12th Fail's message, reflecting the protagonist's relentless pursuit of his dream.
